OpenStudy

As the only designer at ed-tech startup OpenStudy, I was responsible for user research, interaction design, visual design, and usability testing. In this case study, I briefly show how I led the OpenStudy user experience design from zero to over 150k users, from 2000 plus schools, spanning more than 150 countries.

Auditory Menus Research

As part of the Georgia Tech Sonification Lab‘s research team on Auditory Menus, I looked at ways to communicate the unavailability of menu items through sound, using whispers.

This paper was published in the Journal of Audio Engineering society in 2012. A work-in-progress version was published as an advanced abstract in CHI 2010.
